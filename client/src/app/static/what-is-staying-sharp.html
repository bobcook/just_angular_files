Staying Sharp™ is an online tool providing a holistic approach to brain health. It’s filled with science-based information and activities and provides a personalized path to support you in building brain fitness.

Rooted in science, and backed by the Global Council on Brain Health*, Staying Sharp was developed to deliver science-based information, insights and advice to keep your brain healthy. Ninety-three percent of Americans polled say that brain health is vitally important to them, yet were not certain on what they could do to support their own brain health.

Staying Sharp’s holistic approach focuses on five pillars

  • Move

    Move your body to engage your mind.

  • Discover

    Challenge your brain through continuous learning.

  • Relax

    Keep stress levels down to allow your brain to relax and recharge.

  • Nourish

    Feed your brain the foods and nutrients it needs to function best.

  • Connect

    Make social connections to feel vital and engaged and help to keep your brain active.

Articles and games are available for any visitor to the platform, and subscribers to Staying Sharp get access to additional customized tools:

  • Brain Health Assessment

    Your brain health journey begins with a 20-30 minute Brain Health Assessment that measures how your brain works and how your lifestyle supports optimal brain performance.

  • Personalized Brain Assessment Report

    See a detailed report highlighting how your lifestyle supports brain health and how you perform across six measurements of neuroperformance

  • Recommended Activities and Challenges

    Simple brain-healthy changes you can easily fit into your everyday routine are recommended especially for you based on the results of your Brain Health Assessment

  • Activity Tracker

    Stay motivated by tracking your progress towards brain fitness
