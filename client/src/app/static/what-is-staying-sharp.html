Staying Sharp is Holistic

93% of Americans surveyed say brain health is vitally important to them, but few know the five established ways to maintain or improve it. That's why we partnered with scientists to create Staying Sharp, the first holistic approach to brain health personalized to fit your life.

Staying Sharp is Personalized

Your brain health journey begins with a 20-30 minute Brain Health Assessment that measures how your brain works and how your lifestyle supports optimal brain performance. Once you take the assessment, you will receive a detailed report that provides insight on how your brain performs and how your lifestyle supports your brain health.

The report includes activities chosen specifically to fit your lifestyle and brain health needs. You can save the activities that interest you most and get started right away, tracking your progress as you go.

By integrating these small, simple changes in your daily life, you can help improve cognitive function over time.

Staying Sharp is Powered by Science

Like the assessment, Staying Sharp activities, articles, and recipes are supported by science. Research has identified five pillars of health that foster a well-rounded brain-smart lifestyle. You can explore resources within all five pillars, which include:

  • Move

    Move your body to engage your mind.

  • Discover

    Challenge your brain through continuous learning.

  • Relax

    Keep stress levels down to allow your brain to relax and recharge.

  • Nourish

    Feed your brain the foods and nutrients it needs to function best.

  • Connect

    Social connections help you feel vital and engaged.
