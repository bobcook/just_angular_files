Maze Test

You will see a grid of dots.

There is a hidden path connecting the dots.

The hidden path starts at the yellow dot on the bottom row.

The path ends at the blue dot on the top row.

Your task is to FIND AND REMEMBER this hidden path.

You find this path by pressing the UP, DOWN, LEFT and RIGHT arrows on your keyboard (see picture).

You will be shown a green tick when you make a correct move or a red cross when you make an incorrect move.

The path is always the same.

Every time you get to the blue dot, you will be asked to start again at the yellow dot.

If you find the path twice in a row with no mistakes, the task will end.

Click START to begin the test.
