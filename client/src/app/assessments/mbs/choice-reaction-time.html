Choice Reaction Time Test

In this test, you will use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard.

Place your left pointing finger on the LEFT arrow key.

Place your right pointing finger on the RIGHT arrow key.

Two black circles will appear after you start.

When the LEFT circle turns green, PRESS the LEFT arrow key as FAST as possible.

When the RIGHT circle turns green, PRESS the RIGHT arrow key as FAST as possible.

Click START to begin the test.
