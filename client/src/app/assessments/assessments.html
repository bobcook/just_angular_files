About the Brain Health Assessment

MyBrain Assessment is a scientifically validated tool to assess how your brain functions. It is powered by Brain Resource® & the Brain Resource® International Database. Your results from the assessment will be automatically uploaded and scored against your peers in the database (same age, gender & education level).

To take the assessment, you need:

  • 30 minutes of uninterrupted time
  • One of our supported browsers
    • Chrome
    • Safari
    • Firefox
    • Internet Explorer
