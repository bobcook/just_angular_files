Holistic Approach to Brain Health

Research suggests that playing games isn't enough to sustain a truly healthy brain. Instead, Staying Sharp includes resources and things to do across 5 areas:

  • Move

    Studies show that even small amounts of regular exercise like walking can positively impact brain health.

  • Discover

    Research shows that trying new things, like learning a language or starting a new hobby, strengthens our brains.

  • Relax

    Several studies indicate sleep and stress management improve brain health.

  • Nourish

    Science shows that certain elements in food – from omega-3 fatty acids to vitamin E – can positively impact brain health.

  • Connect

    Research shows that staying socially connected to other people supports a healthy brain.
