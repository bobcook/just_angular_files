Move: Studies show that even small amounts of regular exercise like walking can positively impact brain health.

Discover: Research shows that trying new things, like learning a language or starting a new hobby, strengthens our brains.

Relax: Several studies indicate sleep and stress management improve brain health.

Nourish: Science shows that certain elements in food – from omega-3 fatty acids to vitamin E – can positively impact brain health.

Connect: Research shows that staying socially connected to other people supports a healthy brain.
